TF2 Sourcemod Archive

An ambitious project to archive every publicly accessable build of every Team Fortress 2 Sourcemod that we can find, in order to preserve their history. Some mods may not be hosted due to DMCAs.

Browse the Archive

Hosting TF2 Sourcemods Servers

A project to keep actively maintained servers in every possible Team Fortress 2 Sourcemod to help preserve history and playability of every Team Fortress 2 sourcemod with playable builds.
